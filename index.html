MENTAL HEALTH COUNSELING & CONSULTING

“Hope, Help, and Healing — Your Journey to Better Mental Health”

Welcome to MHCC, we are an outpatient mental health clinic located in Las Vegas, dedicated to helping adults improve their well-being and live healthier, more fulfilling lives.

Our team provides comprehensive evaluations to understand your unique strengths, challenges, and needs. From there, we create a personalized treatment plan using evidence-based approaches—meaning everything we do is backed by the latest scientific research. This ensures you receive care designed to give you the best possible outcomes.

What We Offer

  • Individual and Couples Counseling for challenges related to problem gambling, compulsive sexual behavior, depression, anxiety, adult ADHD, and other mental health concerns.
  • Education and Resources to help you better understand mental health and practical ways to improve well-being.
  • Specialized Evaluations for individuals experiencing legal or personal difficulties connected to gambling behavior. We also provide evaluations for substance abuse, ADHD, compulsive sexual behavior, and general mental health.
  • Treatment Grounded in Research to reduce distress, strengthen coping skills, and support long-term recovery.
  • Ongoing Research Efforts that expand understanding of problem gambling, compulsive behaviors, ADHD, depression, anxiety, addiction, and other mental health related issues.

We also use this site as a place to share helpful information for the community—so whether you are seeking services, supporting a loved one, or simply learning more about mental health, you’ll find valuable resources here.

What to Expect at Your First Visit

Taking the first step toward counseling can feel overwhelming, but we’re here to make the process as comfortable and supportive as possible.

Here’s what you can expect when you come in:

  1. A warm welcome – From the moment you arrive, our staff will greet you and guide you through the process so you feel at ease.
  2. Confidential paperwork – You’ll complete a few forms that help us learn about your history, current concerns, and goals for counseling.
  3. A one-on-one conversation – During your first session, you’ll meet with a licensed professional who will listen carefully and get to know you. This is a safe, judgment-free space to share what’s on your mind.
  4. Assessment and planning – We’ll talk about your unique challenges and strengths. Together, we’ll outline a plan that fits your needs and gives you a clear direction forward.
  5. Next steps – Before you leave, we’ll review your options for ongoing sessions and answer any questions so you feel confident moving ahead.

Our Logo and Its Meaning

Our logo represents the journey of change and growth that takes place as each patient works toward becoming the best version of themselves. Each element reflects a part of the therapeutic process:

Sun

The sun symbolizes light in all its forms—clarity, perspective, and insight. Light reveals the path forward, guiding us toward self-understanding, personal growth, and positive change.

Balanced Stones

The balanced stones represent the effort and dedication it takes to build a solid foundation for life. With this foundation, individuals are able to build balance through developing coping skills, emotional intelligence, becoming more mindful, and learning healthier ways of thinking. Empowered with these skills, values and priorities can be clarified creating stability that supports healing and lasting change.

Tree and Leaf

The tree and leaf symbolize the new life and ongoing growth. Just as a tree thrives through consistent care, personal growth flourishes when we nurture ourselves with self-care, meaningful change, wise choices, and healthy practices. With consistent attention to these principles, the healing process can take root and grow stronger over time.

Individual Colorful Leafs.

The individual with hands outstretched and many leaves represents continued growth, with the colors representing all the variety of ways we can continue to experience change in the transformation to becoming the best version of ourself.

The most important day of your transformation is today!
