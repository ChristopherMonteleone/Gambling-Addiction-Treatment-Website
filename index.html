MENTAL HEALTH COUNSELING & CONSULTING

Hope, Help, and Healing — Your Journey to Better Mental Health

Welcome to MHCC, we are an outpatient mental health clinic located in Las Vegas, dedicated to helping adults improve their well-being and live healthier, more fulfilling lives.

Our approach empowers the individuals we serve by encouraging them to better understand their unique strengths, challenges, and needs. We offer a personalized treatment plan using evidence-based approaches—meaning everything we do is backed by the latest scientific research. This ensures our clients receive consistent, high-quality care for the greatest possible outcomes.

What We Offer

  • Individual and Couples Counseling for challenges such as problem gambling, high stress, financial pressure, trauma, anxiety, adult ADHD, and other mental health concerns.
  • Diagnostic assessments to help develop insight and a better understanding of specific challenges.
  • Support and referrals for individuals experiencing legal or personal difficulties related to behaviors such as gambling or other impulse control issues.
  • Individualized planning to help you strengthen executive functioning and cognitive skills, and develop emotional awareness, resilience, and problem-solving abilities.
  • Tools and strategies to reduce stress, strengthen decision making, coping skills, and communication effectiveness.
  • A supportive, non-judgmental environment to process difficult topics, heal, and rediscover hope and happiness.

We know reaching out for help can be overwhelming, but we’re here to make the process as comfortable as possible.

Here’s what you can expect as you explore options with us:

  1. Warm welcome – From the moment you arrive, our staff will greet you and guide you through the process so you feel at ease.
  2. Confidential paperwork – You’ll complete a few forms that help us learn about your history, current concerns, and goals for counseling.
  3. A one-on-one conversation – During your first session, you’ll meet with a licensed professional who will listen carefully and get to know you. This is a safe, judgment-free space to share what’s on your mind.
  4. Assessment and planning – We’ll talk about your unique challenges and strengths. Together, we’ll outline a plan that fits your needs and gives you a clear direction forward.
  5. Next steps – Before you leave, we’ll review available resources and services, and make sure you feel prepared and supported moving forward.

Our Logo and Its Meaning

The sun symbolizes light and clarity, perspective, and insight. Light reveals the path forward, bringing warmth, understanding, personal growth, and resilience.

The human figure with hands outstretched represents our clients as each patient works toward balance and discovery of their own best strengths. Each person is unique, but together we create a supportive community devoted to growth.

Tree and Leaf

The tree and leaf symbolize the new life and ongoing growth. Just as a tree thrives through consistent care, personal growth flourishes when we nurture ourselves with self-care, meaningful change, wise choices, and healthy practices. With consistent attention to these principles, the healing process can take root and grow stronger over time.

Individual Colorful Leafs

The individual with hands outstretched and many leaves represents continued growth, with the colors representing all the variety of ways we can continue to experience change in the transformation to becoming the best version of yourself.
